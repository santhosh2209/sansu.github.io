Best Beaches in the World

Discover the most beautiful beaches to visit !

1. Whitehaven Beach

Located in the Whitsunday Islands, Australia, known for its stunning white silica sand.

2. Anse Source d'Argent

A picturesque beach in Seychelles, famous for its granite boulders and turquoise waters.

3. Bora Bora

An island in French Polynesia, known for its stunning lagoon and luxurious overwater bungalows.

4. Playa Paraiso

A beautiful beach in Cuba, known for its crystal-clear waters and soft white sands.

5. Navagio Beach

Also known as Shipwreck Beach, located in Zakynthos, Greece, famous for its stunning cliffs and shipwreck.
